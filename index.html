Our Story

Authentic Taste of Arifwala

Saj Cafe & More is a popular restaurant in Arifwala known for its BBQ, Karahi, fast food, and traditional Pakistani dishes. Customers love the smoky flavor of our **Koyla Desi Murgh**, **Mutton Karahi**, and **Beef Karahi**.

Beyond tradition, we serve crispy Fast Food, grilled fish, pasta, and aromatic Biryani. Whether you crave a cup of strong tea or a full feast, Saj Cafe is your destination.
